The boys from Liverpool that changed the world of music

Paul McCartney

In voice and bass, Paul was the co-founder of the Beatles, with his incredible partnership with John Lennon, Paul wrote acclaimed songs that became hits all over the world, such as "Hey Jude" and "And I Love Her"!

John Lennon

Beatles founder Lennon was a musical genius from a very young age, having a skiffle band called "The Quarrymen" when he was at school. He had a great career when he was with the Beatles, but also an impeccable solo career with songs like "Imagine" and "Oh my Love".

George Harrisson

George is the Beatles Guitarrist, he didn't have many songs wrote just by him in The Beatles musical literature, but had some titles like "While my guitar gently weeps". He was a close friend of Eric Clapton and an adept to hinduism. He is mine favorite beatle too!

Ringo Starr

Ringo wasn't the Beatles' first drummer, that was Pete Best, Ringo was the second, he didn't write any songs for the Beatles' discography on his own. Ringo learned to play the drums in the hospital where he was when he was a kid, as he was often ill.
